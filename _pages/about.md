Dr. Wei Lu is a Wu Yuzhang Distinguished Professor, serving as vice dean of the School of Information. He is an Executive Committee Member of the Database Technical Committee of the China Computer Federation (CCF) and the Technical Lead of the Database Track of the National Collegiate Computer Systems Ability Competition. His research interest includes database theory, distributed database systems, and data governance. He has published more than 60 papers in top-tier international conferences and journals, with total <a href='https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=v04EazgAAAAJ'>google scholar citations <strong><span id='total_cit'>1500+</span></strong></a> (You can also use google scholar badge <a href='https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=v04EazgAAAAJ'><img src="https://img.shields.io/endpoint?url={{ url | url_encode }}&logo=Google%20Scholar&labelColor=f6f6f6&color=9cf&style=flat&label=citations"></a>). Dr. Wei Lu has served as the principal investigator for multiple national- and provincial-level research projects, including sub-projects of the National Key R&D Program of China, General Programs of the National Natural Science Foundation of China, and major projects funded by the Beijing Municipal Government. He places strong emphasis on research translation and has maintained long-term collaborations with leading database enterprises.
